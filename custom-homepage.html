UNC Dataverse is a data repository service for the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Published data in the UNC Dataverse are publicly accessible and freely available.

Featured

Galápagos Science Center

UNC-Chapel Hill and Universidad San Francisco de Quito in Ecuador collaborated to promote science and education that will help protect the Galápagos Islands' fragile ecosystems and enhance the lives of their inhabitants.

The Love Consortium

A community of researchers is engaged in the advanced scientific study of high-quality social connections. Members of The Love Consortium aspire to facilitate this research through collaborative opportunities that arise from archived data.

Highway Safety

The CSCRS advances road safety by integrating diverse disciplines and a systems approach. Its Data Repository offers research datasets and reports, with some files requiring contact with project representatives for access.
